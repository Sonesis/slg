Lets Generate

Generate multiple links of a particular series in one click

[Currently supports series from O2tvseries for now]

  • Procedure:

  • 1 Get the download link of an episode of your series (it ends with .mp4 or .3gp)
  • 2 Copy and Paste it above then set the max episodes links to generate
  • 3 Hit the 'Generate' button to generate more episodes download links
